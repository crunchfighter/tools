Key Considerations

No Garage
Every comparable sale includes a garage. Buyers in the $355,000–$410,000 price range will raise this consistently. The $10,000 downward adjustment is conservative; buyer perception may discount more aggressively. Marketing should emphasize the half-acre lot and storage shed as partial offsets.
Tax Record Discrepancy
The Portsmouth city tax record lists 3 bedrooms and 2,850 sq ft, which conflicts with the 2020 MLS data of 4 bedrooms and 2,750 sq ft. An appraiser will typically start with the tax record. Recommend contacting the city assessor's office to correct this before going to market.
Comp Size Gap
The subject at 2,750 sq ft is meaningfully larger than all four comparables (range: 1,255–1,931 sq ft). GLA adjustments are significant, which reduces confidence in any single adjusted value. A walkthrough and additional data may surface better comps.
Strong Equity Position
Purchased June 2020 for $260,000 via VA financing. At the recommended list price of $395,000, gross appreciation is approximately $135,000 over six years — a 52% increase. This provides meaningful flexibility on pricing strategy.
